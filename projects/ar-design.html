§01

OVERVIEW

Semester-long AR product design challenge through Berkeley SCET. Team developed an augmented reality feature for Disney pin trading, designing the user experience from research through pitch-ready prototype.

AR Product Design SCET
§02

CONCEPT

Disney pin collectors trade physical pins at parks and events, but verifying authenticity and discovering trade matches is difficult. Our AR concept overlays pin information, rarity data, and trade-matching suggestions in real time through the phone camera.

§03

PRODUCT DESIGN

  • User flow from camera scan → pin identification → trade recommendation
  • Figma prototypes for AR overlay UI and collection management
  • Competitive analysis of existing pin trading apps and AR commerce tools
  • Interaction design for in-park, bright-light outdoor conditions
§04

PITCH & OUTCOME

Final concept pitched directly to the startup CEO as part of the SCET AR Startup Product Design Challenge. Received feature coverage on the Berkeley SCET website for innovative approach to blending physical collectibles with AR discovery.
